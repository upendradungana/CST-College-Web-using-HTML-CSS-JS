College of Science and Technology

The College of Science and Technology (CST), Royal University of Bhutan, is dedicated to nurturing innovative minds and fostering an environment of excellence in science and technology. Established to develop highly skilled professionals, CST offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide quality education and research opportunities, preparing students to contribute to the technological advancements of the nation. We envision a future where CST stands at the forefront of innovation and development.
