Admission Process

The College of Science and Technology offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Admission is based on merit, entrance examinations, and eligibility criteria set by the Royal University of Bhutan. Applicants are encouraged to review the requirements and submit their applications before the deadline.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Minimum qualification: Class XII (Science/Engineering background preferred)
  • Entrance examination for certain programs
  • Application submission with required documents
  • Merit-based selection process
