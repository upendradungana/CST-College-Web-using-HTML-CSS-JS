Research at CST

The College of Science and Technology is committed to fostering a culture of research and innovation. Our faculty and students engage in cutting-edge research projects that address real-world challenges in engineering, technology, and sustainability.

Key Research Areas

  • Renewable Energy and Sustainable Development
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Smart Infrastructure and IoT
  • Environmental Engineering and Water Resources
  • Advanced Materials and Nanotechnology
  • Cybersecurity and Data Science

Collaborations & Funding

We actively collaborate with universities, industries, and government agencies to drive impactful research. Our projects receive funding from national and international organizations, ensuring that our research remains at the forefront of technological advancements.
